Getting Started

The educational content that will be in my video game is all about the past, present, and emerging digital technologies, as it involves the player to answer certain questions. If they get it right, the opponent gets attacked, and if the opposite occurs, the player loses health, and the game will tell them what it correct, meaning that they get educated about the digital technologies. My game incorporates more than one of each past, present, and emerging digital technology, as well as information about the effect that those technologies have had on an individual level, as well as on society as a whole.

User Guide: How to Play

  • Newfio is a game focused on testing your knowledge of digital technologies.
  • To start the game, click on "How to play?" to learn the rules and objectives.
  • Clicking on "Start" initiates the gameplay, bringing you face-to-face with your first enemy, a Drake.
  • To defeat Newfio, you must correctly answer a series of three sets of questions that become progressively more challenging.
  • Incorrect answers result in losing health, but you also learn the correct answers.
  • Between battles, there are scenes where you must locate the next enemy to continue the game.
  • Upon successful completion, you receive a special reward for your efforts.
